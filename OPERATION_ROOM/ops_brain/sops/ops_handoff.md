# SOP: Ops Handoff

## Outcome

Transfer ownership between operations streams without loss of context.

## Steps

1. Define source stream, target stream, and transfer deadline.
2. Attach required artifacts and unresolved risk list.
3. Confirm acknowledgment from receiving stream.
4. Mark handoff status and follow-up owner.

## Evidence

- Handoff manifest
- Risk list
- Receiver acknowledgment
- Follow-up assignment
