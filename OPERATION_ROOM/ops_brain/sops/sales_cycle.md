# SOP: Sales Cycle

## Outcome

Move a qualified opportunity from intake to closed-won with full evidence trail.

## Steps

1. Capture lead source and qualification notes.
2. Confirm scope and commercial fit.
3. Build offer package and timeline.
4. Send proposal and track decision window.
5. On close, trigger fulfillment handoff packet.

## Evidence

- Opportunity intake record
- Proposal artifact
- Close decision note
- Handoff packet to fulfillment
