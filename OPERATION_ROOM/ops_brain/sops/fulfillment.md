# SOP: Fulfillment

## Outcome

Deliver committed scope on time with measurable quality and customer acceptance.

## Steps

1. Receive signed handoff packet from sales cycle.
2. Validate dependencies and execution windows.
3. Execute delivery checkpoints and quality checks.
4. Record completion evidence and acceptance status.
5. Publish completion summary to archive and feedback rooms.

## Evidence

- Handoff packet
- Delivery checkpoint logs
- QA and acceptance record
- Final completion summary
